Language requirement No formal test required. Basic Swedish conversation assessed informally at interview. Mandatory Swedish language test. First test: autumn 2027 (delayed). NEW
Civics knowledge No formal test. Basic understanding of Swedish society expected informally. Mandatory civics test (samhällskunskapstest). First test: August 2026. NEW
Income / self-sufficiency No income requirement. Financial situation not assessed. Min. SEK 20,850/month gross (3 × income base amount = SEK 250,200/year). Must not have received income support for more than 6 months in the 3 years prior to application. NEW
Conduct requirement Must have led an orderly (skötsam) life. No debts with the Enforcement Authority. Stricter "honourable lifestyle" (hederligt levnadssätt) requirement. Crime waiting periods significantly extended — e.g. 4-year prison sentence = 15-year wait before citizenship eligibility. STRICTER
Permanent residence Required for main-rule applicants. Permanent residence on asylum grounds valid. Still required. Note: separate proposal (not yet law) would revoke asylum-based permanent residence from 2027 — register citizenship intent by 31 Dec 2026 to preserve rights. WATCH
Children (under 18) Processed with parent's application or by notification. New specific rules for children. Conduct requirement from age 15. Knowledge requirement from age 16. Consent required from age 12 in certain cases. NEW
Pending applications Assessed under rules in force when decided. Any application not decided before 6 June 2026 will be assessed under new rules. Applying early does not guarantee old rules apply. CRITICAL

Plain language

The income figure is 3 × the income base amount (inkomstbasbelopp). For 2026 this is SEK 83,400 × 3 = SEK 250,200/year. This figure is updated annually by the government. The income must come from salary or self-employment. Certain subsidised employment types do not count. The ability to support yourself must not be only temporary. Inkomstfiguren är 3 × inkomstbasbeloppet. För 2026 är detta 83 400 kr × 3 = 250 200 kr/år. Inkomsten måste komma från lön eller egenföretagande. Viss subventionerad sysselsättning räknas inte. رقم الدخل هو 3 × مبلغ الأساس الدخل. لعام 2026 هذا 83,400 كرونة × 3 = 250,200 كرونة/سنة. يجب أن يأتي الدخل من الراتب أو العمل الحر. Дохід = 3 × базова сума доходу. На 2026 рік це 83,400 × 3 = 250,200 крон/рік. Дохід повинен бути від зарплати або самозайнятості.

  • Check your residence start date. Folkbokföringsregistret is the official record. Log in via skatteverket.se with BankID to see your registration date.   • Confirm you have a permanent residence permit. Check your permit card or log into Migrationsverket's Mina Sidor. You cannot apply for citizenship without it. Bekräfta att du har permanent uppehållstillstånd. Kontrollera ditt tillståndskort eller logga in på Migrationsverkets Mina Sidor. تأكد من حصولك على تصريح إقامة دائم. تحقق من بطاقة تصريحك أو سجّل الدخول على Mina Sidor.   • Verify your income. Check that your gross salary or self-employment income meets SEK 20,850/month. Check your payslips and Skatteverket income records for the past 3 years.   • Start SFI or language preparation now. Even though the language test is delayed to autumn 2027, building Swedish beyond basic SFI level takes time.   • Apply as soon as you qualify — do not wait. Given the 47-month processing time, every month of delay pushes your decision further into the new-rules era. Apply the day you meet the residency and permit requirements. Ansök så snart du kvalificerar — vänta inte.
